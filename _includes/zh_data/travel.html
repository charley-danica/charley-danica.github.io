Travel

Plane

The closest airport is the Belgrade Nikola Tesla airport (BEG). From there to Novi Sad, it's about 1 hr by taxi (~60-100€; ask the driver first and only use vehicles where the license plate ends in "TX"!) or about 1.5hrs and €10 with public transport. Google maps works quite well, and for example, you can take the 607 to Tošin bunar (all buses are free in Belgrade), and then any train to Novi Sad (~10€).

Car

There's plenty of parking all over Novi Sad, including free parking in front of the venue (for a limited number of cars).

Bus

There are bus connections from all over Europe to Novi Sad (and via Belgrade).
